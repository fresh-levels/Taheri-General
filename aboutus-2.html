WHO WE ARE

We Have 25 Years Experience

Our Roots and Commitment

Founded in year 2010, Taheri General Traders Hardware has been a steadfast presence in Mariakani. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision - to be more than just a hardware store. We aspired to become an integral part of our community, offering not only products but also expertise, guidance, and unwavering support to every customer who walked through our doors.

Quality and Expertise

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality and expertise. We believe that every project, whether it's building a dream home or tackling a small repair, deserves the very best. Our team of experienced professionals has handpicked every product on our shelves, ensuring they meet the highest industry standards.

Community and Partnership

Our journey has been shaped by the wonderful people of Mariakani-'Kilifi'. We've had the privilege of being part of countless projects, from building homes to renovating businesses. We understand the unique needs and aspirations of our community, and that's why we've evolved and expanded our offerings over the years to cater to your ever-changing needs.

Our Vision for the Future

As we look to the future, our vision remains clear. We will continue to be your trusted partner for all your hardware and construction needs. We will keep investing in the best products, the latest tools, and the brightest talent to serve you better. Our commitment to quality, expertise, and community will remain at the forefront of everything we do.
